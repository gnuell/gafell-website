Our Story

Founded in 2015, Gafell began by innovating with organic soybean pasta products, successfully exporting to Europe, the United States, and Hong Kong markets. In 2016, we established our food factory in Hong Kong, expanding into meal production and comprehensive central kitchen services.

Over the past decade, we've built strong partnerships with hotels, restaurants, food brands, cafe chains, and entertainment venues across Hong Kong. Our expertise spans from recipe development to final product delivery, offering complete food production solutions.
